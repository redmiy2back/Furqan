Hifz, guided like a real teacher

This is the most ambitious part of Furqan, and it's being built carefully so it actually works: hear a reciter, repeat the verse back, get told exactly which word needs work, and pair verses together the way a teacher would — not a quiz, a lesson.

How it will work

  1. Pick a surah and starting verse.
  2. Listen to a reciter play it, in parts if it's long.
  3. Recite it back — Furqan listens and checks your recitation against the verse.
  4. If a word is off, that exact part replays, and you try again.
  5. Once it's fluent, the next verse joins in, and you recite both together.
